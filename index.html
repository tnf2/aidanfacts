Fact #2
Naturally occurring yellow snow is often considered a delicacy in Denmark and some parts of the Netherlands. This rare snow occurs when the Cortinarius Koldingensis mushroom is covered in snow. As a defense tactic it releases yellow liquid believing it is stunning a predator.
